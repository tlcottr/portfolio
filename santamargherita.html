Santa Margherita Wines
Follow The Vine
Activation, environmental and digital design for Italian winemaker brand, Santa Margherita’s experiential campaign, “Follow The Vine” across 10 US markets.
My Contributions
RSVP site build, Comms system integration, Floorplans, Social asset design
Strategy
Santa Margherita needed a custom RSVP platform that fit into the ecosystem of the national campaign we were launching. Attending guests needed a central location for event info and to register themselves and guests. We created a simple microsite that ushered the user throught the signup process, as well as a communication system for follow up emails.

Additionally, the site needed to exist across all screen sizes, especially tablet size - so that guests could sign up at the event if they hadn't done so prior.
Art Direction
The direction for the campaign was rooted in the European art deco styles of the 1920's. Santa Margherita was founded in the 1930's, which rooted our decision.
UI Animation
Although the site is decidely simple, we elected to create interest through animation. This results in a sort of ethereal feel to each page.
Form Design
Sign-up forms can cause decision inertia if too much information is required. We made it super simple by asking for only the most essential information.

Using Mailchimp, we were able to link IDs from our HTML forms to our Mailchimp Campaigns, creating a neccesary comms system for the campaign.
Outcomes
Overall, we received nearly a 50% sign up rate based on site visitors. Additionally, of the 6,138 visitors, nearly half requested to be added to the Santa Margherita mailing list for future product offers.
