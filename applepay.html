Overview
In 2020, most our lives were upended pretty drastically. For small businesses, however, the COVID pandemic threatened to take everything. Factory360 has a long history of working with Apple Pay for experiential activations, so we began an initiative to bring commerce back to small businesses.

We created promotional incentives for those using Apple Pay in order to Pay Safer, and even provided signage kits to any participating small business that wanted one.
Custom Templates
We ultimately went with a simple custom html template that could be hacked into the Outlook web client for sending. This saved time and effort over going with a dedicated CMS.

Additionally, this also meant that I was able to rapidly prototype and test styles on the fly, which streamlined our design process.
Signage
Finally, we created multitudes of signage for the benefit of small business owners. Brand partnerships ranged from local brands to corporate giants.
