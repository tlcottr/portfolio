Overview
Speaking in sign language is more personal than other languages. Speaking languages do not require you to be face to face, or even see the individual you are speaking with. ASL requires that level of vulnerability as a baseline.

I began learning sign language around the same time I began learning 3D modeling and rendering. Besides being an unusual combination, I've been able to retain learnings from both much better.
