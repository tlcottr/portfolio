Overview
E-textiles are emerging as the next evolution of wearable technology. By integrating the data collection process into Nike's performance apparel, athletes and consumers alike will obtain more clarity from each workout.

However, in order to make this data relevant to athletes or consumers, a platform must be built to harness and anaylze it.
